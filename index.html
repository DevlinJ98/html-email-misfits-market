What to order this week

Given how well they pair together in meals, it may come as a surprise that potatoes and onions are mortal enemies on your kitchen countertop. Why? Onions release ethylene gas and moisture that causes potatoes to ripen faster—and sprout those unsightly eyes. They may taste delicious when cooked together, but in order to help both veggies last a long time, store them in cool, dry, dark areas apart from one another. (When stored properly, potatoes can last about two weeks at room temperature; onions will last up to two months.)
